# Amelco US Market Context

## Company Overview
Amelco is a UK-based B2B betting technology provider. Founded 2007.
Offices: London, Warsaw, Pecs (Hungary).
Regulated by UK Gambling Commission.

## Product Capabilities
- Full iGaming platform (proprietary, built in-house)
- Sportsbook (nearly two decades of operational excellence)
- Casino aggregation (100,000+ titles from top studios)
- AI and personalization engine
- Trading services (in-house trading teams + data division)
- Player Account Management (full lifecycle)
- Mobile apps (award-winning, custom-built)
- Risk management tools
- White label solutions (fast deployment)
- Sports models and data feeds
- Retail and omni-channel (kiosks, POS, single-wallet)
- Bespoke development

## Current US Footprint
- 17 US states
- 50+ global operators
- 15+ tribal partners

## Strategic Differentiators for US Market
1. Pure B2B - does not operate consumer-facing sportsbooks or
   prediction markets (clean positioning with tribal partners)
2. Tribal gaming vertical with dedicated solutions
3. UK heritage = deep fixed-odds horse racing expertise
4. Modular architecture allows tribes/operators to use full
   platform or individual components
5. Retail/omni-channel capability for brick-and-mortar + online
6. Already serves Flutter and Fanatics, proving tier-one scale

## Current Strategic Priorities (Update as needed)
- Expand tribal gaming partnerships (especially in states
  adopting hub-and-spoke models)
- Position for California 2028 tribal sports betting push
- Target new state launches as white-label/platform provider
- Leverage fixed-odds horse racing capability in states
  authorizing it (Kentucky, others)
- Build relationships with mid-tier operators entering new markets
